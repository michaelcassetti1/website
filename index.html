HOME GYM BUILD

With the COVID-19 lockdown, gyms immediately shut down and gym equipment became few and far between. Every store was sold out and Craigslist saw 300% spikes in prices. So, I decided to build my own home gym. For around $300, I built a squat rack out of wood that included a full cable pully system, adjustable bench press and squat functions, leg press station, pull-up bar, weight storage, and 1 inch rubber horse stall mats around the perimeter. The construction is solid with zero rattle, and the weight pegs can easily support over 600 pounds without budging.
